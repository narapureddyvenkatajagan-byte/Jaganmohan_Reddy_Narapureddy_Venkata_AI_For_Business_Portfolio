## About This Portfolio

Throughout this course, I learned how machine learning can support business decision-making by identifying patterns in data, generating predictions, and helping organizations make more informed choices.

The hands-on projects gave me experience with important artificial intelligence and machine learning concepts, including classification, support vector machines, decision trees, dimensionality reduction, artificial neural networks, and natural language processing.

---

## Skills Developed

During these projects, I strengthened my ability to:

* Prepare and preprocess datasets
* Split data into training and testing sets
* Train and evaluate machine learning models
* Measure model accuracy and performance
* Tune model parameters
* Create meaningful visualizations
* Interpret results for business use
* Select appropriate models based on data quality, business needs, and explainability

I also learned that choosing the right machine learning model depends not only on accuracy, but also on the business problem, the available data, and how easily results can be understood by users and decision-makers.

## Reflection

These projects helped me understand the complete machine learning process, from exploring a dataset to training models and evaluating their results. Each project showed how artificial intelligence can be applied to real business challenges.

Through this course, I gained a stronger understanding of how AI can support forecasting, customer analysis, process automation, operational improvement, and data-driven decision-making in organizations. I am now more confident in applying machine learning concepts to practical business situations.
